Integrations

OAuth handles every connection. Tokens stay on your device. Read-only by default · I never write to your accounts.

about OAuth scopes

I ask for the minimum read-only scope each service supports. Gmail metadata + message bodies. Calendar event list. Drive file listing only. I never request "send mail" or "manage calendar" scopes. Your token goes from Google to your browser to my IndexedDB. Nobody else sees it. You can revoke any time in your Google security settings.
